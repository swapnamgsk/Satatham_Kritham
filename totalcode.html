ABOUT US

Satatham Kritam Legal Solution LLP

Satatham Kritam, together, means “Continuum of actions or creations of excellence”. The name of the Firm is an inspirational and logical basis for building the culture of Satatham Kritam Legal Solution LLP (SK). Satatham Kritam Legal Solution LLP (SK) was incorporated on 3rd December 2024 as a Limited Liability Partnership (LLP) firm.

Satatham Kritam (SK) brings a unique offering to the practice of IP in India by collaborating with innovators and innovative companies to build a strategy for the protection of their valuable IP right from the very beginning of the creation of their innovation, product, and services strategy. SK collaborates with clients to create an IP protection strategy by planning and conducting IP harvesting sessions, helping in-house counsels identify high-value inventions, and assisting inventors in creating high-quality invention disclosures for these inventions.

Further, SK collaborates with inventors and innovative companies to devise an effective IP protection strategy to safeguard their valuable IP. SK's offerings include preparing high-quality patent applications, prosecuting patent applications, preparing claim charts, and protecting trademarks, designs, and copyrights in cutting-edge technology areas such as semiconductors, wireless, software, artificial intelligence (AI), quantum, mechanical, robotics, standard essential patents, and other inter-disciplinary technology areas.
